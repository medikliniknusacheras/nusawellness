FAQ / 常见问题

Q: What are IV therapies? / 问：什么是IV治疗？

A: IV therapies involve the administration of vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients directly into the bloodstream for rapid absorption and effectiveness. / 答：IV治疗是指将维生素、矿物质和其他营养物质直接输送到血液中，以便快速吸收和有效发挥作用。

Q: What are the benefits of IV therapies? / 问：IV治疗有哪些好处？

A: Benefits include improved energy levels, enhanced immune function, better skin health, and faster recovery from illnesses. / 答：好处包括提高能量水平、增强免疫功能、改善皮肤健康以及更快地从疾病中恢复。

Q: Are there any side effects? / 问：有没有副作用？

A: Side effects are rare but may include allergic reactions, infection at the injection site, or vein inflammation. It's essential to consult with a healthcare provider before starting treatment. / 答：副作用很少见，但可能包括过敏反应、注射部位感染或静脉炎。开始治疗前咨询医疗提供者是很重要的。

Q: How effective are IV therapies? / 问：IV治疗有多有效？

A: Many patients report positive effects, but scientific evidence is mixed. Some studies support the benefits for specific deficiencies, but more research is needed to confirm widespread efficacy. / 答：许多患者报告了积极效果，但科学证据是混合的。一些研究支持对特定缺乏症的好处，但需要更多研究来确认广泛的效果。

Q: How long does an IV therapy session take? / 问：IV治疗疗程需要多长时间？

A: Most IV therapy sessions take between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the type of infusion and individual patient needs. / 答：大多数IV治疗疗程需要30到60分钟，具体时间取决于输液类型和个人需求。

Q: How often should I get IV therapy? / 问：我应该多久进行一次IV治疗？

A: The frequency of IV therapy depends on your individual health goals and needs. Some people benefit from weekly sessions, while others may only need monthly treatments. Consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best plan for you. / 答：IV治疗的频率取决于您的个人健康目标和需求。有些人每周进行一次疗程会受益，而其他人可能只需要每月治疗一次。请咨询医疗提供者以确定最适合您的计划。

Q: Is IV therapy safe? / 问：IV治疗安全吗？

A: IV therapy is generally safe when administered by trained professionals. However, it is important to discuss your medical history and any concerns with your healthcare provider before starting treatment. / 答：IV治疗在经过培训的专业人员的操作下通常是安全的。然而，在开始治疗之前，与您的医疗提供者讨论您的病史和任何担忧是很重要的。

Q: Can anyone get IV therapy? / 问：任何人都可以进行IV治疗吗？

A: Most people can benefit from IV therapy, but it may not be suitable for everyone. Those with certain medical conditions or allergies should consult with a healthcare provider to ensure it is safe for them. / 答：大多数人都可以从IV治疗中受益，但并非每个人都适合。有某些医疗状况或过敏的人应咨询医疗提供者，以确保对他们是安全的。
