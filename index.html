Vitamins

Vitamins are essential nutrients that play a crucial role in maintaining the health of our body. They support various functions including immune response, energy production, and skin health.

Vitamin A

Vitamin A is important for vision, the immune system, and reproduction. It also helps the heart, lungs, kidneys, and other organs work properly.

Vitamin A maintains our health by improving vision, enhancing immunity, and helping in tissue regeneration.

Vitamin B1 (Thiamine)

Vitamin B1 is crucial for glucose metabolism and plays a key role in nerve, muscle, and heart function.

It helps maintain our health by supporting energy production, enhancing nerve function, and boosting immunity.

Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine)

Vitamin B6 is involved in protein and glucose metabolism and is crucial for the synthesis of neurotransmitters and hemoglobin.

This vitamin helps in liver detoxification, improving mood, and supporting overall metabolic health.

Vitamin B12 (Cobalamin)

Vitamin B12 is essential for brain health, nervous tissue health, and the production of red blood cells.

Vitamin B12 helps maintain our health by supporting energy levels, brain function, and red blood cell formation.

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is an antioxidant that helps protect cells from damage, aids in the absorption of iron from plant-based food, and supports the immune system.

It boosts immunity, promotes healthy skin, and aids in detoxifying the body.

Vitamin D

Vitamin D helps to regulate the absorption of calcium and phosphorus, and facilitates normal immune system function. It is important for the development of bones and teeth.

Vitamin D supports bone health, enhances mood, and boosts the immune system.

Vitamin E

Vitamin E acts as an antioxidant, helping to protect cells from the damage caused by free radicals. It is important for immune function and skin health.

It helps in maintaining healthy skin, boosting immunity, and providing anti-aging benefits.

NAD+

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every cell of the body. It plays a key role in energy metabolism and is essential for the proper functioning of metabolic processes.

NAD+ helps to maintain our health by boosting energy levels, supporting cellular repair, and improving cognitive function.

Placenta

Placenta therapy involves the use of placenta extract to rejuvenate and repair tissues in the body. It is believed to have anti-aging properties and can improve skin texture and vitality.

Placenta extract supports tissue regeneration, boosts mood, and provides anti-aging benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the benefits of IV therapy?

IV therapy delivers vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients directly into the bloodstream, allowing for faster and more efficient absorption. Benefits include increased energy, improved mood, enhanced immune function, better hydration, and detoxification.

Are there any contraindications to IV therapy?

IV therapy is generally safe, but it may not be suitable for individuals with certain health conditions such as kidney disease, heart conditions, or electrolyte imbalances. It is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting IV therapy.

What kind of screening is required before starting IV therapy?

Prior to starting IV therapy, a comprehensive health screening is recommended. This may include blood tests to assess kidney and liver function, electrolyte levels, and overall health status. A consultation with a healthcare provider will help determine the most appropriate IV therapy based on individual needs.

How long does an IV therapy session take?

An IV therapy session typically takes between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the type of therapy and individual needs. Some therapies may take longer.

How often should I get IV therapy?

The frequency of IV therapy sessions depends on individual health goals and the specific therapy being administered. Some people may benefit from weekly sessions, while others may only need therapy monthly or as needed.

What should I expect during an IV therapy session?

During an IV therapy session, you will be seated comfortably while a healthcare professional administers the IV drip. The process is generally painless, although you may feel a slight pinch when the needle is inserted. You can relax, read, or use your phone during the session.

Are there any side effects of IV therapy?

IV therapy is generally well-tolerated, but some people may experience minor side effects such as bruising, redness, or swelling at the injection site. More serious side effects are rare but can include allergic reactions or infections. It's important to have IV therapy administered by a qualified healthcare provider.
